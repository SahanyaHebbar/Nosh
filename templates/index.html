What is NOS#?

A platform that allow invitees to rsvp anonymously and give the organizers a better estimate of the total number of attendees for an event.

Create Event

Register your event and obtain a unique Event ID. Circulate this Event ID amongst your invitees and ask them to head over to nosh.com, enter the event ID and anonymously enter the number of attendees for the event from their side. You can view your event headcount before placing the food order.

Unique ID

Invited for an event? RSVP anonymously using the given Event ID and let the organizers know the number of people attending the event from your side. Do your part in making the world a better place now!

NGO Partners

Got surplus food after the event? Contact our NGO partners and have them pick up the excess food to distrubute amongst the needy, which is a great way to improve the conditions in your neighborhood or community. Donate and do your part in countering poverty, hunger.
